Sekyu Base

A Filipino team game where you protect your base and rescue your teammates.

Introduction

Sekyu Base is a Filipino outdoor game played by two teams. The goal is to protect your own base while trying to tag the opponent's base and rescue captured teammates. Players need to work together and be quick to win the game.

image caption(kay wla pa man pic di lang danay).

How to Play

  1. Each team has a base and players must protect their base.
  2. Tagging the opponent's base and rescuing teammates is key to scoring.
  3. If a player is tagged, they are captured at the opponent's base until rescued by a teammate.

Tips

  • Assign a player to guard your base while others attack.
  • Work together to rescue captured teammates quickly.
  • Use strategic movements to confuse the opposing team.

Langit Lupa

A fun Filipino chasing game involving "heaven" and "earth."

Introduction

Langit Lupa is a classic Filipino children's game that involves a chaser ("taya") and other players running between elevated ("langit") and ground-level ("lupa") areas. Players aim to avoid being tagged by the taya while moving to new positions after each chant.

image caption(kay wla pa man pic di lang danay).

How to Play

  1. Determine the "taya" by using a chant like "Langit, lupa, impyerno."
  2. Players start on the ground ("lupa") and run to elevated areas ("langit") to avoid being tagged.
  3. The taya cannot tag players on elevated positions, but players must move after each chant ends.
  4. The tagged player becomes the new taya, and the game continues.

Tips

  • Stay alert and ready to move when the chant ends.
  • Find the closest elevated spot to avoid being tagged.
  • Coordinate with other players to distract the taya.

Bente Uno

A Filipino tag game where players become "it" and tag others.

Introduction

Bente Uno is a Filipino children's game where one player becomes "it" and tags others. Once tagged, the tagged player becomes "it" too. The game continues until the last player is tagged and performs an action to become "it."

image caption(kay wla pa man pic di lang danay).

How to Play

  1. The first "it" starts by tagging other players.
  2. Once tagged, the new "it" joins in tagging others.
  3. The last player tagged must perform an action (e.g., jump, spin) and becomes the new "it."

Tips

  • Stay quick and unpredictable to avoid being tagged.
  • Use your environment for obstacles and distractions.
  • Cooperate with others to tag the last player quickly.

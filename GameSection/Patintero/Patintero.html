Patintero

A traditional Filipino game of agility and strategy.

Introduction

Patintero, also known as Harangang-Taga, is a Filipino outdoor game. It involves offensive players trying to cross lines guarded by defensive players without being tagged. The game is won by getting players through the grid without being caught.

image caption(kay wla pa man pic di lang danay).

How to Play

  1. Set up a rectangular grid with lines to guard.
  2. Offensive players attempt to cross the grid without being tagged by defensive players.
  3. If tagged, the offensive player must go to the back of the line.

Tips

  • Cooperate with teammates to cross lines without being tagged.
  • Defensive players should stay aware of offensive players' movements.
  • Practice defensive strategies to improve team coordination.
